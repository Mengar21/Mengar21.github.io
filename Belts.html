Taekwondo Belt Levels

In Taekwondo, you will be awarded a series of belts as you progress towards a black belt… and beyond! Below are the belt levels used at our Taekwondo school. However, many Taekwondo school have their own belt levels and color systems so please check with your Taekwondo instructor.

No Belt – You start without a belt and after passing your first belt test, you are awarded a white belt.

    • When And How?

    Students generally have a color belt test every 3 months (until they reach the higher color belt levels). They will be tested for knowledge of and their skill in specific forms, kicks, breaks and self-defense techniques. Moreover, other schools also test students on additional subject matters (i.e. Taekwondo history and terminology).

    Please see your Taekwondo instructor to learn what will be tested at your school for your next belt test. After achieving a black belt, it takes more and more time to achive the next belt.E.G to go from First Dan to Secoond Dan you must wait at lease 2 year, to go from Seccond Dan to Third Dan you must wait at least 3 years. And the pattern continues.

    Remember that many Taekwondo schools use different color belt systems so the color belt system above is just a sample of what you might find if you join a Taekwondo school.

    Black Belt Levels – Time to achieve these belts can vary at different schools.

  • 1st Degree/Dan Black Belt – This initial Taekwondo black belt takes about 3-4 years to earn (after passing all of the color belt tests and a black belt test)
  • 2nd Degree/Dan Black Belt – Generally 2-3 years to earn.
  • 3rd Degree/Dan Black Belt – Generally 3-4 years to earn.
  • 4th Degree/Dan Black Belt – Generally 4-5 years to earn.
  • 5th Degree/Dan Black Belt – Generally 5-6 years to earn.
  • 6th Degree/Dan Black Belt – Generally 6-7 years to earn.
  • 7th Degree/Dan Black Belt – Generally 7-8 years to earn.
  • 8th Degree/Dan Black Belt – Generally 8-9 years to earn.
  • 9th Degree/9th Dan Black Belt – If you reach this level, you are a Taekwondo Grand Master. This is generally the highest belt level in the WTF Taekwondo system awarded to “living” people.
